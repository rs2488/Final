About Me

My name is Rowan Schreiber, and I am a college student born and raised in Madison, Wisconsin. I grew up in a family of four with my mom, dad, and younger brother Beckett. My family has had my brother and I in sports from a young age, as we have always been at least a head taller than all our peers. My two main sports growing up were swimming and volleyball, and I was planning to swim in college after my 10 years competing if not volleyball. When I'm not busy working hard as a student-athlete at NJIT, I enjoy creative hobbies like crocheting, scrap-booking, journaling, drawing, jewelry-making, painting, and graphic designing. I love cats, going to see live music, and taking pictures.
